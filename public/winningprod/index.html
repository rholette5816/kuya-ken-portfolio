PART 4

Delivery Success vs Failed Orders

The line graph shows exact Pancake counts per month. Green is delivered orders, red is returned or returning orders.

Delivered orders Failed orders

Peak volume created the most failures

September and October had the most delivered orders, but also the most failed parcels because order volume was highest.

December had the weakest fail rate

Only 110 orders were delivered, but 76 failed. That is the clearest month where fulfillment and COD follow-up hurt collection.

No dedicated CX support

Limited confirmation, address validation, and delivery follow-up allowed weak COD orders to enter shipping.

April improved the failure ratio

April had 95 delivered and 18 failed orders, the best failure rate in this period at 14.5%.

Format: delivered / failed. Failed means returned or returning Pancake orders. Partner opportunity: add CX filtering and fulfillment control to protect COD delivery success.
