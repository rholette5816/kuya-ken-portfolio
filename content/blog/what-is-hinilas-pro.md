Running Facebook Ads for a product business requires a constant supply of fresh angles, copy variations, and creative direction. Most small businesses in the Philippines cannot afford a dedicated copywriter. Most founders do not have the time to write their own ad copy from scratch for every campaign.

Hinilas Pro exists to solve that problem.

## What It Is

Hinilas Pro is a web-based tool that generates ad angles, copy, and creative briefs for product-based businesses. You input your product, your audience, and your goal. It outputs structured ad copy variations, angle breakdowns, and image prompt direction you can use immediately.

It is built specifically for the Philippine eCommerce context: COD funnels, Bisaya and Tagalog buyer psychology, Facebook-first traffic, and the kind of product categories that actually sell here.

## Why I Built It

I have managed Facebook Ads for multiple product brands across Visayas and Mindanao. The bottleneck in almost every campaign is not the targeting or the budget. It is creative production.

Coming up with a new angle, writing copy that speaks to a specific pain point, generating a brief for a designer or image AI tool. These tasks take hours when done from scratch. Done at scale across multiple products and campaigns, they consume enormous amounts of time.

I built Hinilas Pro to make this process fast. Not to replace the human judgment about which angle to run. To give you the raw material to test, iterate, and find what works without starting from a blank page every time.

## Who It Is For

Hinilas Pro is built for Philippine eCommerce sellers running or planning to run Facebook Ads.

Specifically: solo operators managing their own campaigns, small brand owners who cannot afford a dedicated copywriter, and marketing assistants who need structured creative output faster than they can produce it manually.

It is not built for large agencies managing dozens of accounts with full creative teams. It is built for the business owner in Cebu or Davao who needs to produce good ad copy today and does not have four hours to sit down and write it.

## What It Produces

For any product you input, Hinilas Pro generates: multiple ad angles based on buyer psychology, complete ad copy variations for each angle, image prompts you can use with AI image generators, and a brief summary of the target buyer profile.

All of this is exportable and usable immediately in your ad campaigns.

## Where to Start

Hinilas Pro is available now. If you are running product ads in the Philippines and spending more time on copy than on strategy, it is worth trying.
